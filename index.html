Shardhana Project

Open-source exploration of the Applied Element Method (AEM)

About the Project

Shardhana is an open-source initiative aimed at developing a computational framework for structural analysis based on the Applied Element Method (AEM). Unlike traditional finite element methods, AEM provides a more natural way to simulate structural behavior, including failure mechanisms, collapse analysis, and progressive damage.

Our Vision

The vision of Shardhana is to create a free, collaborative platform where engineers, researchers, and students can explore advanced analysis techniques. We believe that structural simulation tools should be both scientifically rigorous and accessible to everyone.

  • Implement AEM fundamentals in a transparent, open way
  • Encourage community contributions and shared learning
  • Support both research and educational applications

Get Involved

Shardhana is hosted on GitHub, where you can browse the code, report issues, or contribute improvements. Whether you are an experienced developer, a researcher, or a student eager to learn, your participation is welcome.
