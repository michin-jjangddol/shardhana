🏗️ Shardhana Project

Shardhana is an open-source structural analysis software based on the Applied Element Method (AEM). This project aims to bring together engineering expertise, open collaboration, and modern computing for accurate and scalable structural simulation.

🎯 Purpose

  • Make AEM accessible to a global engineering community
  • Encourage open collaboration in structural analysis software
  • Bridge academic research and real-world engineering
  • Foster innovation through shared knowledge and tools

🚀 Vision

Our goal is to create a platform that supports designing every structure — not only on Earth, but also in space. Like air, Shardhana should be free and open for everyone.
